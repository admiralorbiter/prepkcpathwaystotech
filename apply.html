P2T Applications

Applications for the Pathways to Technology open at the beginning of the Spring semester. Look for an application for the 22-23 school year around January 2022.

You must be a part of a partipcating high school: Grandview High School, Ruskin High School, East High School, Center High School

Requirements:

  • 2.5+ GPA
  • 90% Attendance Rate
  • Enrolled or passed Algebra 2

After applications close, you will be contacted by PREP-KC to discuss your application.
