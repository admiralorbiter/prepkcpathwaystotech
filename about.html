About Pathways to Technology

This program provides high school juniors and seniors in the Kansas City metro hands on experience in a variety of technology fields.

  • Computational Thinking
  • Python Programming
  • Web Dev - HTML/CSS/JS and Bootstrap
  • Data Science and Visualizations
  • Arduino and Circuits

Students complete projects in the various skills in the fall, then complete for internships in the spring.
